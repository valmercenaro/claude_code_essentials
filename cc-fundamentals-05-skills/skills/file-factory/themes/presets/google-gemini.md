# Google Gemini

A vibrant, modern theme based on Google's brand colors with Gemini AI purple accents. Clean, accessible, and recognizable.

## Color Palette

- **Google Blue**: `#4285F4` - Primary brand blue
- **Google Red**: `#EA4335` - Alert and emphasis
- **Google Yellow**: `#FBBC04` - Warning and highlights
- **Google Green**: `#34A853` - Success and positive
- **Gemini Purple**: `#8E24AA` - AI/Gemini accent
- **Deep Blue**: `#1A237E` - Dark text and headers
- **Banana Yellow**: `#FFE135` - Special highlights
- **Light Gray**: `#F5F5F5` - Backgrounds
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Clean backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: Google Sans, Arial, Roboto
- **Body Text**: Roboto, Arial
- **Code/Data**: Roboto Mono, Consolas

## Recommended Complexity

Level 2-6 (Clean Professional to Ultra-Extreme)

## Design Elements

- Four-color Google palette for variety
- Clean, material design-inspired layouts
- Rounded corners on elements
- Gradient effects in extreme versions
- Stats bars with multiple colors
- Comparison tables using brand colors

## Best Used For

Google product documentation, Gemini AI content, Android development, Material Design projects, cloud services documentation.

## Color Usage Pattern

Use the four Google colors strategically:
- Blue: Primary actions, links, main headers
- Red: Alerts, important callouts, warnings
- Yellow: Highlights, attention-grabbing elements
- Green: Success states, positive metrics, confirmations
- Purple: AI-specific content, Gemini references
